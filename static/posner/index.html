Posner Cueing Task

Welcome to the Posner Cueing Task! This experiment will test your attention and reaction time.

You will see a central fixation cross (+) in the middle of the screen.

Keep your eyes focused on this cross throughout the experiment.

A cue (arrow) will briefly appear, pointing either left or right.

Shortly after, a target (*) will appear on either the left or right side.

Your task is to press the arrow key (← or →) that corresponds to the side where the target appears.

Respond as quickly and accurately as possible!

Note: The cue is not always predictive of where the target will appear.
