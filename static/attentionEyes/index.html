Eye Tracking Attention Experiment

In this experiment, you will watch pairs of videos and answer questions about them.

The videos show the attention path of a person using a white spotlight overlayed on an image.

One video is modeled from the attention path of a real person. The other is of a computer agent.

The experiment will be split into two phases. Each phase will have 10 trials. There is a break between the two phases.

For each trial:

  1. You will watch Video 1
  2. Then you will watch Video 2
  3. After both videos, you will answer two questions:
    • Which video shows the attention path of a real person?
    • How confident are you in your judgment?
